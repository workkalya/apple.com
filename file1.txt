- Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company.
- It was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976.
- Apple is known for its innovative products such as the iPhone, iPad, and Mac computers.
- The company's headquarters is located in Cupertino, California.
- Apple operates retail stores worldwide and has a strong online presence.
- The company is also known for its software products, including macOS, iOS, and watchOS.
- Apple places a strong emphasis on design and user experience.
- The company has a large and loyal customer base.
- Apple is one of the world's most valuable companies by market capitalization.
